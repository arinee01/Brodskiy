About

This website was developed within the course “Organization and Digital Methods in the Cultural Heritage Domain” at the University of Bologna (2025).

The project explores the life and cultural legacy of Joseph Brodsky through semantic modeling, metadata encoding, and Linked Open Data technologies. It showcases ten carefully curated heritage items — from texts and photographs to personal artefacts — semantically described using international standards.
